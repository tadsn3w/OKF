RFC 2119 defines the meaning of capitalized requirement keywords used in
the OKF spec (SPEC.md) and project rules. The full text is at
`standards/conventions-used-rfc2119.md` in the project root.

## Key definitions

- **MUST** — absolute requirement
- **MUST NOT** — absolute prohibition
- **SHOULD** — recommended, valid reasons may exist to ignore
- **SHOULD NOT** — not recommended, valid reasons may exist to do
- **MAY** — truly optional

SPEC.md uses these keywords in §4 (frontmatter), §5 (cross-linking),
§6 (index files), and §9 (conformance).
